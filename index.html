Key Components

This section will describe the each core component of Manulife POC

  • Header: You'll see quick menu and navigating to other pages with ease from here.
  • Login: Identify yourself by inputting email address and password to gain an access to the system.
  • Check Claim: Check your claim status and claim details by inputting claim number and taxonomy code.
  • Claim Details: Your claim details are presented here.
  • Footer: It contains of quick links, such as about us, careers, privacy policy, and site map. Copyright statement is also mentioned here.
